Chris, like Jill, starts the franchise as a rookie thrust into a nightmare one could only dream of. Over time, he hardens his shell into a seasoned soldier, bearing the physical and emotional scars of his battles. He grapples with the many complexities of fighting bioterrorism, sometimes forced to make difficult choices like ending a few lives to save the many. Chis is cursed to the trope of usually being the last man standing and has endured many falls from it but he has always come back to do what is right to protect those who cant protect themselves even at a high cost. This is what made him so interesting and my favorite protagonist of the franchise, although he started off as a cookie-cutter horror protagonist he grew into a fantastic character to watch and play as.
