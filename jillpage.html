Jill, like Chris, is a rookie set into her worst nightmare. She always sticks to her guns and is able to be calm and collected. Jill fiercely protects those she cares about, this loyalty adds depth to her personality and showcases her compassion. This loyalty is what also gets her hurt on various occasions. She grapples with PTSD and the fear of becoming a monster herself, adding a layer of vulnerability to her strength. Despite the trauma she endures, Jill never gives up hope for a better future. Jill continues to fight for the world's safety, inspiring others with her determination. She embodies the mental and emotional toll of heroism while showcasing unwavering courage and a will to survive.
